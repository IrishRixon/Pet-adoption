Our web app is designed for users who have many pets but cannot support them due to their living conditions and pet lovers that want to adopt adorable pets. They can post their pets on our web app and look for potential adopters who are interested in giving them a new home. Our web app also allows users to chat with each other and arrange the details of how and when they can meet up.
